October 2022- November 2022

The training aimed at enhancing the understanding of participants on the intersection of data protection and gender rights, ensuring that data practices are aligned with principles of inclusivity and respect for gender-related privacy concerns.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Developed a customized training curriculum that covers fundamental data protection concepts with a specific emphasis on gender-related considerations.
  • Designed interactive and participatory training sessions that facilitate engagement and knowledge exchange among participants.
  • Incorporated case studies and examples that highlight the impact of data protection on diverse gender groups.
  • Addressed the specific challenges and opportunities associated with data protection in the context of gender rights.
  • Provided practical tools and guidelines for integrating gender-sensitive data protection measures into organizational policies.
  • Conducted pre-training assessments to tailor the content to the participants' level of understanding and organizational context.
